If you are currently pursuing grants funded through the NIH stimulus money, Ingenuity may be able to help.


IPA can accelerate your grant-funded research

We are excited to share with you that IPA and related Ingenuity services are eligible for the $200M of grant funding provided through the NIH Challenge Grants in Health and Science Research. The NIH stimulus grant program provides funding for the purchase of new software, technologies, and tools to address knowledge gaps and research opportunities. For more details on specific grant areas, go to  http://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/challenge_award/.

 

Why IPA?

IPA is the trusted industry standard for discovery research and has been cited in more than 1400 peer-reviewed publications. IPA and IPA-related analysis services are well positioned in many of the specified Challenge Grant areas, including Enabling Technologies, Genomics, Biomarker Discovery, and more.

 

Let us help you with your grant application

We have received requests from many of our IPA users to provide assistance on the inclusion of IPA licenses and other Ingenuity services in their submissions. If you are an existing IPA customer or new to Ingenuity, and you are interested including IPA licenses and related services in your grant submission, we can work with you to provide information and supporting materials.


Take advantage of funding with your existing IPA license

Given the NIH requirement to disburse the full $200M in grant funding within an 18-month period, and to receive grant applications by April 27, 2009, many of our customers are currently choosing to purchase additional licenses of IPA and/or renew their licenses for multiple year terms to take advantage of this funding period. Please contact your account manager if you are interested in renewing your IPA license now.


Next steps

For application submission guidelines, please go to http://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/rfa-files/RFA-OD-09-003.html. If you are interested in having Ingenuity provide supporting materials for your grant application, please contact your Ingenuity account manager or send us an email at support@ingenuity.com. We will respond to your request within 24 hours. Please note that the application is due April 27, 2009.


Best of luck in your grant applications!
