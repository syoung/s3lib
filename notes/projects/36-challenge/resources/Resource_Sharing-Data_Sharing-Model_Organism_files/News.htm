March 30, 2009 - 2009 NIH Salary Cap and Stipends for Training Grants and Fellowships:

NIH has recently confirmed the new salary cap and announced new training grant stipend levels for 2009.

  • Effective 1/1/09, salaries can be paid from NIH grants up to the new salary cap of $196,700.  For further detailed information, click here.
  • Effective for awards made on or after 10/1/09, NIH NRSA training grant/fellowship stipend levels have increased but amounts for training related expenses have not changed.  For further detailed information, click here.
  • Additional NIH fiscal policies and legislative mandates for 2009 can be found here and here.

 

March 6, 2009 - Stimulus Package Offers Special One-Time Funding for PIs:

The Stimulus Package (SP) provides many oppotuinities for obtaining new research resources, as well as an unprecedented opportunity for UCSD principal investigators (PIs) to secure federal funding. The timeline for responding to these opportunities in the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act (ARRA) is short, as the intent is to stimulate the nation's economy. To access Stimulus Package daily updates, click here.

 

The Office of Contract and Grant Administration (OCGA) is prepared to review and submit these fast-track proposals; modify and resubmit earlier proposals that may now be more favorably received; and request supplements to existing awards; and is committed to ensuring that the process proceeds as smoothly as possible. Submission will be via the federal government's Grants.gov Web site. We expect traffic on this Web site to be extremely heavy, so PIs are urged to finalize proposals for submission by OCGA a few days prior to their deadline dates. For further detailed information, click here.

 

January 16, 2009 - NIH Raises Salary Cap:

NIH announces updated information regarding the salary limitation for NIH grant and cooperative agreement awards and extramural research and development contract awards. Effective January 1, 2009, the Executive Level 1 salary cap will be $196,700. For further detailed information, click here.

 

January 5, 2009 - NSF Mentoring Requirement for Postdoctoral Scholars:

Effective January 5, 2009, NSF proposals that request funding to support postdoctoral scholars must include, as a separate section within the 15-page Project Description, a description of the mentoring activities that will be provided to the postdoctoral scholar. Please Note: Proposals that do not include this separate section on mentoring within the Project Description will be returned without further review.

  • For further detailed information on mentoring activities, click here.

  • For further detailed information on mentoring guidelines, click here.

  • To access the NSF Project Description section within the OCGA Web site for new proposals, click here.

January 2, 2009 - Join us at Sharecase to learn about the latest offerings from OCGA:

On Wednesday, March 25, 2009, stop by the Office of Contract and Grant Administration (OCGA) booth at Sharecase to meet OCGA staff members, to learn about the new OCGA Web site, to inquire about the eLearning classes that OCGA offers, as well as many other research related tools and resources that are available through OCGA.
