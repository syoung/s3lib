Job Description


Worcester Polytechnic Institute
Biology and Biotechnology and Mathematical Sciences Departments
Tenure Track Assistant Professor in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology

The Biology and Biotechnology and Mathematical Sciences Departments of WPI invites applications for a tenure track Assistant Professor position with a research focus in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, to begin in the fall of 2012. Supported by the new Life Sciences and Bioengineering Center (LSBC) at Gateway Park, a state-of-the art, interdisciplinary research facility that opened Spring 2007, WPI is committed to further strengthening its capabilities in the Life Sciences arena. Applicants should have a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics/Computational Biology, Biology, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related area, and postdoctoral research experience. Supported by the exceptional research environment at the LSBC, the successful candidate is expected to develop a robust, externally funded, high profile research program and should participate in cross-disciplinary research in bioinformatics and computational biology. Resources for startup funding are competitive and commensurate with research experience and accomplishments.

Founded in 1865 and located one hour west of Boston, WPI is one of the nation's oldest technological universities and a highly selective private university. To learn more about the Departments, graduate and undergraduate programs, the LSBC, and WPI visit www.wpi.edu/+BBT, www.wpi.edu/+Math,  www.wpi.edu/+BCB, and www.wpi.edu.

Requirements:
Applicants should submit a single pdf-formatted file including a cover letter, curriculum vitae, a statement of accomplishments and future priorities in research and teaching, and a list of five references (with contact information). Applications should be sent to Bioinformaticsfaculty-search@wpi.edu. Review of applications will be conducted on a rolling basis and continue until the position is filled.

To enrich education through diversity, WPI is an affirmative action, equal opportunity employer.
